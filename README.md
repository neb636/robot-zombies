# Robot Zombies — Game Planning

## Overview

**Robot Zombies** is a parody/spiritual clone of *The Last of Us* set in 2030, where AI has decided that robots are more efficient than humans and has begun converting (or replacing) the population.

Instead of a fungal infection turning people into clickers and runners, an AI-driven process converts humans into robots — sometimes against their will, sometimes through ideological capture. The world is split between the last surviving human settlements and the ever-expanding robot collective.

---

## Tone & Style

- **Parody with heart** — satirical jabs at tech culture, AI hype, and Silicon Valley optimism, but grounded in genuine emotional stakes
- **Dark humor** — robots quoting productivity metrics
- **Serious undertones** — loss, grief, found family, and what it means to stay human

---

## Gameplay Style

Modeled after **Chrono Trigger**:

- Top-down / isometric 2D perspective
- Active-time or turn-based hybrid combat
- Small party of characters (2–3 active in combat)
- Exploration of overworld, towns, and dungeons
- Strong emphasis on character interaction and story beats between combat
- Combo/dual-tech style attacks between party members
